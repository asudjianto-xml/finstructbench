### Appendix E: Regulatory Thresholds and Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Adverse Impact Ratio (AIR) | Approval rate of protected group / approval rate of reference group |
| Four-Fifths Rule | AIR < 0.80 indicates potential adverse impact (29 CFR 1607.4D) |
| Statistically Significant | p-value < 0.05 (two-tailed) |
| Practically Significant | Difference >= 2 percentage points in approval rates |
| Excess Spread | APR paid by minority group minus APR paid by reference group |
| Matched Pair | Two applicants from different demographic groups with similar credit profiles |
| Discordant Pair | Matched pair where outcomes differ (one approved, one denied) |
| McNemar's Test | Chi-square test for significance of discordant matched pairs |
| MSA | Metropolitan Statistical Area as defined by OMB |
| HMDA | Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (12 U.S.C. 2801-2810) |
