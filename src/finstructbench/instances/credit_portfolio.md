## Executive Summary

This report presents the quarterly credit portfolio review for Pacific Continental
Bank, N.A. as of December 31, 2024. The bank maintains a diversified loan portfolio
totaling $18,812.4 million across eight primary lending segments. During
the quarter, total loans grew 4.4312% from the prior quarter-end balance. Key risk
themes for the quarter include rising classified assets in the Commercial Real Estate
Non-Owner Occupied segment, elevated net charge-off rates in the Consumer-Card and
Consumer-Auto portfolios, and increasing industry concentration in Healthcare &
Pharmaceuticals. The Allowance for Credit Losses stands at 1.8309% of total loans,
which management considers adequate given current portfolio composition and economic
conditions.

## Appendix A: Methodology Notes

- **Delinquency Rates** are calculated as the aggregate principal balance of loans
  past due by the stated number of days divided by the total segment balance.
- **Net Charge-Off Rate** is annualized and represents gross charge-offs less
  recoveries divided by average segment balances for the quarter.
- **Classified Assets** include all loans rated Substandard, Doubtful, or Loss.
- **Criticized Assets** include all Classified assets plus Special Mention.
- **Coverage Ratio** is defined as ACL balance divided by total outstanding loans
  for the segment.
- **Migration probabilities** are computed as the proportion of loan balances
  transitioning between risk rating categories during the quarter.
- **Vintage loss rates** represent cumulative net losses from origination through
  the report date, expressed as a percentage of original funded commitments.
- **Peer data** sourced from FFIEC Call Report data (schedule RC-N and RC-R)
  for banks with total assets between $15 billion and $30 billion.
- **Concentration limits** are expressed as a percentage of Tier 1 Capital as defined
  in the bank's Credit Concentration Policy, last amended April 2024.
- **Pass/Fail determinations** use the thresholds established in the Board-approved
  Credit Risk Appetite Statement dated January 2024.
- **Geographic exposure** is determined by the physical location of the primary
  collateral or, for unsecured credits, the borrower's principal place of business.
